Conclusion & Recommendations

Key takeaways and actionable steps to improve student mental health.

Conclusion

What we’ve learned from the data.

  • Stress Patterns: Poor sleep quality and high academic loads significantly increase stress levels.
  • Course Impact: Certain courses, like Engineering, show higher average stress.
  • Protective Factors: Physical activity and social support correlate with lower stress.
  • High-Risk Students: Over 200 students consistently report stress levels above 4.

Recommendations

Actionable strategies for improving student mental health programs.

  • Sleep Education: Offer workshops on sleep hygiene to improve sleep quality and reduce stress.
  • Course-Specific Support: Provide extra counseling for high-stress courses like Engineering.
  • Activity Programs: Encourage physical activity through free gym access or group fitness classes.
  • Real-Time Alerts: Use the dashboard’s high-stress alerts to connect at-risk students with counselors.
  • Peer Support: Build peer mentorship programs to enhance social support networks.
